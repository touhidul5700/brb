Frequently Asked Questions

What types of devices do you repair?

We repair a wide range of electronic devices including smartphones, tablets, laptops, and gaming consoles. This includes major brands such as Apple, Samsung, Sony, and many more.

How long does a typical repair take?

Most repairs are completed on the same day, often within an hour. Complex repairs such as water damage or motherboard issues may take longer. We strive to return your device as quickly as possible while ensuring the highest quality service.

Do you offer a warranty on repairs?

Yes, all repairs come with a 90-day warranty that covers parts and labor. This warranty does not cover accidental damage done after the repair.

Can I book a repair appointment online?

Yes, you can book repair appointments directly through our website. Visit our 'Book A Repair' page to select your device type, describe the issue, and choose a time that works best for you.

What should I do before handing over my device for repair?

We recommend backing up your device data before any repair. Please also ensure that you provide any passcodes or unlock patterns so that we can test the device post-repair.

Is there anything I can try at home before bringing in my device for repair?

For minor issues, such as software glitches, trying a restart or a factory reset (after backing up data) might help. For hardware issues, we advise professional help to avoid any further damage.

Do you use original parts for repairs?

We use OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) parts for most repairs. If OEM parts are unavailable, we use the highest-quality aftermarket parts available.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including major credit cards, debit cards, cash, and mobile payment platforms such as Apple Pay and Google Pay.
